Features

SciLabReserve provides a seamless experience for students, faculty, and administrators with the following key features:

  • Online Reservation - Quickly book science laboratory rooms, equipment, and chemicals.
  • Inventory Management - Track usage and stock levels of lab resources.
  • Conflict Prevention - Avoid double bookings with built-in schedule checks.

How it works:

Step 1:
Sign up or Login

Create an account or log in using your credentials
Step 2:
Check Availability

Use the inventory or schedule view to see available labs, equipment, or chemicals
Step 3:
Make a Reservation

Fill out the reservation form with your preferred date, time, and resource
Step 4:
Wait for Approval

Your request will be reviewed and confirmed by the admin
Step 5:
Use the Resource

Go to the science laboratory at the reserved time and use the approved resource
